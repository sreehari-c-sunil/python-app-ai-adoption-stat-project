About This Platform

Research Project

Title: Analyzing AI Adoption Among College Students: Trends, Impact, and Future Prospects

This platform was built as part of an AI Use Case Research Internship. It serves as a practical tool for analyzing how college students adopt and perceive artificial intelligence tools in their academic and personal lives.

Technologies Used

  • Python / Flask: Web framework and backend logic
  • SQLite: Lightweight database for storing survey data
  • Pandas: Data manipulation and analysis
  • NumPy: Numerical computing
  • SciPy: Statistical tests (Chi-Square, T-Test, ANOVA, Correlation)
  • Statsmodels: Logistic Regression
  • Matplotlib / Seaborn: Chart generation

Persona Focus

Student Persona: Analyzes AI usage frequency, academic performance (GPA), comfort with AI, career interests, ethical concerns, job replacement fears, and optimism about AI's future.

Teacher Persona: Analyzes teacher adoption of AI, use in lesson planning, concerns about academic integrity (cheating), and perceptions of AI improving student learning outcomes.

Research Alignment

This platform directly supports the research internship focus on:

  • Real AI use case testing
  • Persona-based research and analysis
  • Practical observations and documentation
  • Tool testing and evaluation
  • Data-driven decision making
  • Honest statistical evaluation with transparent limitations

Limitations and Disclaimers

  • Statistical significance does not imply causation
  • Results are only as good as the quality and representativeness of the uploaded data
  • Small sample sizes may lead to unreliable statistical conclusions
  • Self-reported survey data can contain bias
  • This tool is for academic research purposes only
